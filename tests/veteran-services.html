Hillsborough County's Veterans Services serves as a single point of contact for assisting all Veterans and their families in accessing federal, state and local benefits. The office staff supports Veteran's activities in the County and connects Veterans to useful resources whether living in assisted living facilities, nursing homes or private residences.

With more than 94,000 Veterans in this area, Hillsborough County has the fourth largest Veteran population in Florida. Hillsborough County Veterans Services assists an average of 30,000 clients each year through personal and telephone interviews, outreach, community presentations, in-home visits, and correspondence. In 2013, Hillsborough County Veterans and survivors received an estimated $323 million in federal benefits, placing it first in the state for economic impact.
