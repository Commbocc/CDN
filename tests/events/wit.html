When: Monday, March 30, 2015
Time: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Where: Seminole Heights Library (See Map)
4711 N. Central Ave.
Tampa, FL 33603

Reserve Your Seat

The free event is part of a collaborative effort to highlight, teach, and inspire young women to pursue careers in technology through shared connections and resources. Join us for a local learning opportunity to highlight, teach, and inspire through shared connections and resources.

This event will include:

  • Tech Meet and Greet
  • Pecha Kucha - Style Presentations
  • Break Out Sessions incllude:
  • Learn to Code!
  • Tech Careers Speed Networking
  • Interactive Panel

Panel members include:

  • Marisol Casablanca, IT consultant at PCNet Corserva
  • Dr. Stephanie Tripp, digital media and associate professor at University of Tampa
  • Stephanie Ashley, director of innovation and incubation programs at University of South Florida Connect
  • Yael Monereau, engineer at National Society of Black Engineers
  • Nina Sanka, systems engineer at Customers Bank
