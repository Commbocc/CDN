Hillsborough County Pet Resource Center, the County's animal shelter and The First Place for Pets, invites the community to the next First Saturday Celebration happening on Saturday, September 5, 2015 from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM at the facility at 440 N. Falkenburg Road in Tampa.

Stop in for family-friendly fun over the Labor Day holiday weekend with our "Raise the Roof" party with $5 adoption specials. All of our dogs, cats, puppies and kitten adoption fees include vaccinations, spaying/neutering, deworming, microchip, Hillsborough County license tag and a tote/goodie bag.

Monthly First Saturday Celebration events began in August 2014 to help attract shelter visitors and adopters with family-friendly fun, music, educational exhibitors and affordable pet specials. Last year, successful community support helped to achieve positive outcomes for more than 13,000 homeless pets -- a landmark success for the County animal shelter.

Date: Saturday, September 5, 2015
Time: 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM
Where: Pet Resource Center.

Hillsborough County's Pet Resource Center is often the first place to receive the community's lost and surrendered animals, and the shelter hopes to be the first place adopters come to select a new pet. The facility is open daily from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM for adoptions, lost pet searches, spay/neuter vouchers, and County pet license tags.

Donations are always welcomed to help needy shelter pets as well as those in care of in-home foster volunteers. For a list of suggested items, visit our Volunteer/Donate page.
