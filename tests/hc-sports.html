Hillsborough County Adult Athletics offers slow-pitch softball for men, women, co-eds, and seniors. Players can chose from three adult softball seasons per year - summer, spring and fall. Players can chose to play at two County locations:

Williams Owens Pass Park

Player Information

The annual fee for teams is $350. A team consists of no more than 20 people.

Teams play one night a week; games are also offered on weekends. Generally a season consist of 9 to 12 games. Games are scheduled depending on the number of teams in your division per season. A schedule of games will be posted online (typically one week before the season starts.)

All teams must attend the organizational meeting to play in the upcoming season. Teams will be notified of meeting date and location. 

Free Agents

Don't have a team? Register online with your location preference and days you prefer to play. County staff will help match you with a team.

Rules

All Hillsborough County adult softball players must follow rules outlined by the 2015 Softball Rules Guide including:

  1. All players must be 18 years of age or older.
  2. All players must sign a seasonal consent wavier of release.
  3. All teams must pay registration fees in full prior to the season start.
Register for Adult Softball

Teams must register online. A $350 fee and signed waiver is required.
