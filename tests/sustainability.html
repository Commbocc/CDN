Hillsborough County Government is committed to efficient, cost effective and environmentally responsible use of energy in County operations. Being an ENERGY STAR Partner, the County benchmarks its facilities using National Medians.

  • Central Chiller Plant with ice storage for air conditioning in our downtown facilities

  • Construct new facilities using LEED standards, as with the Plant City Courthouse and the Tampa Bay History Center

  • Electric Vehicle Charging Stations

  • Compressed Natural Gas Vehicles

  • EArTH - Energy AwaReness Team Hillsborough provides a dashboard to view details and graphs for the County's various facilities. EArTH promotes public awareness of energy use trends, scores, certifications, team leaders and more! View the various County facilities to see how we are doing with energy use. EArTH is maintained and supported by the IIO-DAE team while the content is derived from the Energy Star Portfolio Manager and managed by Real Estate and Facilities.

Annually we are saving over $3,400,000 from our program.
