Since 1965, more than 80,000 kids have participated in Hillsborough County's Head Start program receiving comprehensive services for success in school and in life. To celebrate Hillsborough County will host two events.

Did you participate in Hillsborough County's Head Start program? If so, we want to hear from you. Tweet us your Head Start story! Use the hashtag #50YearsofOpportunity.

Events

Head Start Awareness Month Proclamation

When: Thursday, October 1
Time: 11 AM - 1 PM
Location: Joe Chillura Park, Corner of Morgan, Kennedy, Pierce, and Madison (Across the street from the County Center and Courthouse)

A celebration including a program with Commissioners, County Leaders, a guest speaker, presentation by Head Start children, and a testimonial plus a proclamation commemorating Head Start Awareness month. Light lunch for all attendees will be served after the program.

Head Start 50th Anniversary Party and Reunion

When: Saturday, October 17
Time: 10 AM - 3 PM
Location: Al Lopez Park, 4810 N Himes Ave. in Tampa

Head Start families, past and present, are invited to participate in a day of fun activities. This will include games, bounce houses, food, balloon artists, face painting, guest speakers and more. In honor of our thousands of successful children and families, we will hold a 1K walk honoring their accomplishments.
