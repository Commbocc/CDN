The Office of Neighborhood Relations helps residents enhance and strengthen their communities by providing training, funding and networking opportunities to preserve the quality of life in Hillsborough County neighborhoods.

Ways we help:

  • Assists citizens and groups in developing neighborhood associations
  • Award annual grants to deserving neighborhood organizations
  • Recognize stellar community leaders during the annual Neighborhood Awards
  • Educate residents on best practices during the annual Neighborhoods Conference
  • Coordinate Government Leadership University
