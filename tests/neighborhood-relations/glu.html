Apply To GLU

This 10-week interactive program is designed to give participants insight into the operations of Hillsborough County government. GLU graduates become more-informed residents who share knowledge about the workings of county government with their respective neighborhoods.

Participation in the Leadership University will help you:

  • Get to know county government leaders.
  • Unravel the "red tape" syndrome and clear the path for easier ways to conduct business.
  • Establish relationships with department directors and staff.
  • Explain interactions between County departments.
  • Provide a look at County facilities, such as a water treatment plant.

Standards for Admission:

  • Applicants must be at least 18 years old.
  • Applicants must make a commitment to attend a minimum of 7 sessions.
  • Participants must be a resident of Hillsborough County, a business owner in Hillsborough County, or employed full-time in Hillsborough County.
  • GLU is a learning and information experience. Acceptable conduct standards will be maintained by participants to
  • assure each session is a positive experience.
