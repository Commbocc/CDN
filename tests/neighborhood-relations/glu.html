Go Behind the Scenes

This 10-week interactive program is designed to give participants insight into the operations of Hillsborough County government. GLU graduates become more-informed residents who share knowledge about the workings of county government with their respective neighborhoods.

Government Leadership activities include:

  • Planning for a hurricane evacuation in the face of an approaching storm
  • Listening in on the drama of real 9-1-1 calls
  • Balancing a county budget
  • Site visit to the county’s land fill
  • Participation in a “Mock Board Meeting” – participants will review real zoning cases hear before the county’s Hearing Officer
  • Learn about County Code Enforcement
